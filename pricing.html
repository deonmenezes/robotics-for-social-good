FAQ

Common Questions

Where does my money go?

85% goes to data contributors, 10% funds underserved community programs, and 5% supports platform operations and open-source development.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All subscriptions can be cancelled immediately with no questions asked. You retain access until the end of your billing period.

Is the data open source?

Community-tier datasets are licensed under CC-BY-4.0. Premium datasets have individual licensing set by their contributors.

Do you offer non-profit discounts?

Yes. Verified non-profits and academic institutions get 50% off all paid plans. Contact us with your organization details.
